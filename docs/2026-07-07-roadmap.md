# Conn roadmap

Written 2026-07-07, post stock-take, by the session that shipped the island
personality motion and the app-spawned-daemon fixes. This is the forward
spec: what runs next, in what order, why that order, and what each block
must prove before the next one starts. Packet-level detail lives in the two
execution docs; this file sequences them and records the decisions that
changed since they were written. Every packet here is executable without
Fable: the cold-start notes in the plan name the fable-* skills to load,
and this file carries the judgment calls so a cheaper session does not have
to remake them.

Sources of truth:

- `docs/plans/2026-07-05-ux-craft-plan.md` - island round packets (I-, S-, V-)
- `docs/2026-07-06-capability-spec.md` - capability packets (X-, M-, P-)
- `docs/STATE-OF-PLAY.md` - current state, open stops
- `docs/idea-ledger.md` - everything deliberately not being done, with triggers

## The one-line thesis check

The craft is ahead of the proof. The island is structurally correct, alive,
and fast; what Conn has not yet proven is the product bet, that voice beats
the keyboard for the three target loops (app switching, vault search,
context read-back). Every block below either closes a craft gap that blocks
daily use, or converts an unproven claim into a measured one. When a block
does neither, it waits.

Phase 5: V1 upgrades the checklist and evals (per-task latency columns fed
from receipts, three reliability drills as permanent tasks), then STOP 5,

## Standing constraints (apply to every block)

- Safety invariants are never traded: harness owns permissions, pointer-only
  approvals, continuations withheld until real results, hard budget cap,
  no osascript, empty shell allowlist.
- Every motion or palette literal lives in DesignTokens.swift; the guard
  test is the enforcement.
- Latency is a feature: no block may regress key-down-to-listening, and any
  packet adding a timer or a hot-path hop must say so in its commit.
- Slopcheck clean on every doc and commit message; no em dashes anywhere.
- Solo-Fable execution is the exception, not the norm; packets carry their
  model tier, and deviations get a ledger line.
